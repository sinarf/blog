--- layout: post title: Problem with Word 2007. date: '2008-10-20T17:27:00.000+02:00' author: Michel Blavin tags: - sinarf modified_time: '2011-05-19T07:03:02.356+02:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-7412981599879440541.post-7279161960827147496 blogger_orig_url: http://blog.sinarf.org/2008/10/problem-with-word-2007.html --- At work, we use Office 2007 and I had bunch of problems with. It was impossible to use the mouse to click or select or anything but scrolling.
We were 3 to have the same problems, we finally foud the sollution. We were the only one to have PowerAMC 12.5 installed on our PCs! The problem was the powerAMC plug-in for word. To solve the problem we just had to disable it.
